Community Charter

Trust is the currency of our network.

1. The Principle of Invitation

Entry to a circle is a sacred trust. Members agree to never share an invitation link with a third party without the explicit consensus of the group.

2. Protection of the Vulnerable

This network is a sanctuary for all, especially children and the elderly. We commit to:

  • Maintaining high-trust "invite-only" barriers for family gaming.
  • Reporting known bad actors to the local "cell" for immediate exclusion via consensus.
  • Upholding the safety of those who are marginalized or threatened.

3. No Malicious Coordination

While we defend the right to private assembly, we do not permit the network to be used for the coordination of imminent physical harm or the exploitation of others. Liberty is not a license for malice.

4. Sovereignty & Responsibility

You are the owner of your data and the guardian of your node. By using this system, you accept the responsibility of maintaining the integrity and security of your own digital home.

5. Peaceful Resolution

Disputes within a circle are resolved by the members of that circle. We act with empathy, dignity, and a shared goal of maintaining a peaceful gathering space.
